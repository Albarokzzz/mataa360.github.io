360° Virtual Tours

Explore spaces digitally with our innovative 360 virtual tours.

Mata Projections creates interactive 360° HD virtual tours for various industries, serving as powerful marketing tools for promoting property, services, and facilities. Accessible from smartphones, tablets, and laptops, these tours offer a unique way to showcase your company, providing viewers with an immersive experience.

360° Virtual Tours - features & functions:

| Aerial Panoramas

Create a vast and stunning panorama of the surrounding scenery.

| Embedded Maps

Implement easy acces to your company's physical location with a clear and embedded map.

| Interactive Hotspots

Implement seamless navigation with clickable objects embedded in visual content, customized to suit your needs.

| Motion Hybrid Videos

Amaze your audience with live-action videos and real-time footage seamlessly integrated into your virtual tour.

| Information Buttons

Direct your potential customers to directly interact with your company's information and contact details.

| Background Music

Enhance your audience's experience with background music integrated into your virtual tour.
